A Personal Note from the Developer

Finding Christ in Your Own Way

Finding Christ in Your Own Way

Everyone enters Christ in their own way, through their own path. There is no single road map, no universal formula—only the deeply personal journey that unfolds uniquely for each of us.

I found Christ first through meeting my wife, who shared with me the glory of Jesus. Through encounter after encounter, I discovered that Jesus was always there with me. Real truth doesn't come from the outside—it's been resonating within, just needing to find its song.

The Heart Behind Bible Key

As I built this app, I thought deeply about how to present scripture and how to guide the AI in responding to your questions. I've tried to keep the answers very much neutral, and when it comes to theological matters, to live in the New Testament and the teachings of Jesus.

But here's what's important: The answers may not be the answers you were looking for. They may even conflict with your path to Christ, and that is completely okay.

Grace for Different Paths

Let's give each other grace. If this app isn't for you, that's totally okay. I take no offense.

I made this app for myself—to learn the Bible, to grow closer to Jesus, to have a companion in my journey of faith. It was only as I made it for myself that I thought: maybe this could be good for others too.

We begin with a shared truth: We are one, and Jesus is everything.

From that foundation, our paths may diverge in beautiful and unexpected ways. Your relationship with Christ is yours alone. Your interpretation of scripture, your theological understanding, your spiritual practices—they belong to you and the Holy Spirit working within you.

What Bible Key Offers

This app is a tool, nothing more and nothing less:

  • The Word: The Berean Standard Bible, presented clearly and beautifully
  • A Voice: Professional narration to let scripture wash over you
  • A Companion: An AI assistant trained to reflect on scripture through the lens of Jesus' teachings
  • A Space: For your notes, your highlights, your personal journey with the text

What Bible Key Is Not

This app is not:

  • A replacement for the Holy Spirit's guidance
  • A substitute for Christian community and fellowship
  • The final word on theological matters
  • A tool to convince you of one interpretation over another
  • Perfect or complete

An Invitation

If the way Bible Key approaches scripture resonates with you, I'm grateful to be part of your journey. Use it as a companion, a study tool, a quiet space to encounter God's word.

If it doesn't fit your path, I genuinely understand. The body of Christ is vast and diverse, and there are many beautiful resources out there for every kind of seeker.

Technical Transparency

The AI assistant has been guided to:

  • Ground responses in the teachings of Jesus
  • Emphasize New Testament principles
  • Avoid denominational dogmatism
  • Encourage personal reflection and prayer
  • Point back to scripture as the ultimate authority

But AI is not infallible. It cannot replace your pastor, your spiritual director, your prayer life, or the Holy Spirit's guidance.

My Prayer for You

My prayer is that this app helps you:

  • Encounter God's word regularly
  • Discover Jesus in new and deeper ways
  • Find moments of peace and reflection
  • Grow in your understanding of scripture
  • Draw closer to the One who loves you infinitely

Contact and Community

I'm always open to feedback, questions, and conversations. If something about this app troubles you, enlightens you, or sparks a question—reach out. I'm learning too.

Email: thoughts@biblekey.app

In Closing

Thank you for considering Bible Key for your spiritual journey. Whether this app becomes a daily companion or just a brief stop along your path, I'm honored that you're here.

May you find Christ in your own way, in your own time, through your own beautiful and unique path.

Faithfully,

Ali Momen
